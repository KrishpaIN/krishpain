About SUP

Sustainable Upgrade Program (SUP) is a flagship startup from Hyderabad engaging colleges and schools to take some action against climate change and environmental issues. We are coming up with a fun and interactive mobile application to imbibe Sustainable lifestyle in student’s life showing them the impact they can make for environment and in return rewarding them to take up more actions. At this point we are seeking partnerships in the verticals like retail, hospitality, MNCs etc. Keeping it in mind, we found that your organization could be a part of our program which urges sustainable revolution in India.

SUP is a flagship socio-entrepreneurship start-up working on communicating and engaging youth to take action against climate change and environmental issues. To date, climate change communication has been dry and technical and un-attentive to the general audience. People are not aware of each activity and its impact.

The program’s objective is to help youth in India to evolve and become a part of the increasingly rising green communities to empower future generations to become environmentally responsible citizens by a holistic approach of communication.

We finally have the information technology infrastructure to communicate the climate crisis that happened because of us. So We are coming up with a personal climate action app, users can track their carbon footprint based on their lifestyle. The app gives you personalized tips for you on how you can save money along with climate and reward the user with points price and deals for taking action to fight climate change.

We would like to engage them through an action calculator. The role of this action calculator is, we will list various sustainable actions and the users who will do them in their real life and can add points by themselves for their actions.

The points accumulated will be used to unlock rewards for them. The intention to give them the rewards is to encourage them to do more actions. The rewards are given by our partners who want to give discounts for a greater purpose.
